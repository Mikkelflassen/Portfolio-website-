Short Introduction

"Hi there, I'm Mikkel, an IT-Product Development student at Aarhus University. I love building things that bridge the physical and digital world - whether that's an autonomous tracking robot, an interactive lighting system, or a reimagined board game with LED strips and haptic feedback.

I bring together skills in software engineering, physical computing, and product thinking. My toolkit includes Python, Java, JavaScript, Raspberry Pi, Arduino, and various sensors and actuators. I've also got a background in product management from the fintech industry, which helps me focus on solving real user problems.
